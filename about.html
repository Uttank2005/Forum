Who are we?

Electel is the official forum of the Department of Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering under the Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE). Our mission is to bridge the gap between students and industry experts, fostering innovation, technical excellence, and career growth.

Our Vision

  • Connecting students with industry professionals through guest lectures and industrial visits.
  • Encouraging innovation and research in electronics, telecommunications, and IT.
  • Providing a platform for collaboration, networking, and hands-on projects.
  • Enhancing students' technical and leadership skills through workshops and mentorship programs.
